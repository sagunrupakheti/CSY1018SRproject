Both the companies are the lead in today's technical world and both the jobs are equally rewarding and beneficial. Comparing both the jobs one of them is clearly a win for us. With much more salary and also the welcoming quality of inexperienced skillfull people, Google is a better choice for inexperienced people however, if in hands are 5 years of experience then Apple is also a smart choice. Even though there is not much of a difference in the salaries, the fact that Google is openly believing to accept not so experienced personals is impressive. This shows how skills are more important than experience.
